About Us

At Handy Renos Winnipeg, we are a young company powered by a team with more than 30 years of combined experience across construction and renovation trades.

The company was founded by a Mechanical Designer by profession, bringing a strong technical background in aluminum assemblies, glass solutions, European and North American window and door systems, and structural problem-solving. Combined with years of hands-on renovation work, this foundation allows us to execute projects with precision, durability, and clear professional standards.

Our motto, “Jack of All Trades, Master of Some,” reflects who we are. Each member of our team has a core specialty—doors and windows, structural framing, interior finishing, plumbing, electrical or technical repairs—while remaining highly capable across multiple trades. This versatility keeps our solutions practical, efficient, and cost-effective.

We support property managers, homeowners, landlords, and investors with:

• Patio door restoration and roller/hardware upgrades
• Modernization of older aluminum doors and windows
• Automatic door adjustments & repairs
• Structural modifications: new openings and enlarged openings
• Leveling and subfloor correction for uneven floors
• Painting, drywall, and plaster repair
•masonry and stucco repair
• Full interior renovations (kitchens, bathrooms, basements)
• Kitchen installation and remodeling
• Bathroom modifications and upgrades
• Tile installation on floors, showers, and backsplashes
• Cabinet and countertop installation
• Plumbing and electrical troubleshooting and installation
• General repair and handyman work

Clients rely on us for clear, honest, and technically sound solutions, especially when a job requires structural understanding, creative problem-solving, or a mix of several trades.
