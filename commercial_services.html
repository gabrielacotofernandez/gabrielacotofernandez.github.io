Commercial Services

Handy Renos Winnipeg delivers reliable, cost-effective services for commercial buildings, multifamily properties, retail units, and hotels. Our team brings decades of combined experience addressing technical, structural, and finishing needs in commercial environments. 

Door, Window & Façade Systems:

• Storefront door and window repairs
• Patio door restoration for multifamily units
• Aluminum and glass replacement
• Hollow metal door repairs and hardware upgrades
• Automatic door adjustments & repairs
• Repair of rusted steel door frame sections (cutting out and replacing damaged areas)
• Reinforcement of hinge backplates and hinge areas on metal doors and frames
• Repairs on cracked aluminum frame corners and joints

Interior & Turnover Improvements:

• Drywall repairs and professional wall patching
• Plaster repair in common areas and units
• Painting for suites, corridors, lobbies, and commercial interiors
• Subfloor leveling in units and commercial spaces
• Minor flooring repairs and transitions
• Small kitchenettes or break-room installations
• Bathroom fixture replacement and light plumbing work in commercial units 

Structural & Exterior Work:

• New openings for doors or windows in interior or exterior walls (where structurally feasible)
• Enlarged openings for accessibility, light, or layout changes
• Weatherproofing and exterior sealing improvements
• Basement and lower-level water ingress solutions
• Masonry repair: brick, block, and mortar joints
• Stucco patching and repair on exterior walls

Property Manager Support:

• Preventive maintenance for high-traffic doors and common elements
• Cost-saving repair-first strategies to extend asset life
• Flexible service options to match building needs and budgets
