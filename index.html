Why us?

Handy Renos Winnipeg is a young and fast-growing company supported by a team with decades of combined experience in construction, structural work, doors, windows, and interior renovations.

We specialize in practical, durable, and cost-efficient solutions—from door and window repairs to structural changes, leveling, and complete interior remodeling. Our approach focuses on extending the life of existing components whenever possible, while delivering high-quality finishes when upgrades are needed.

Based in South Winnipeg, we proudly serve Downtown and all surrounding areas.
