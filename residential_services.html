Residential Services

Handy Renos Winnipeg provides dependable, precise renovation and repair services for homeowners, landlords, and real estate investors. Our work combines structural understanding with high-quality interior and exterior finishing. 

Doors, Windows & Exterior Repairs:

• Patio door repairs and roller upgrades
• Door and window repairs or replacements
• Mosquito screen repairs and replacements
• Stucco patching and small exterior wall repairs
• Small masonry repairs around steps, foundations, and window areas
• Repair of rusted metal door frame sections
• Reinforcement or reattachment of hinge plates and strike areas on metal doors
• Repair of cracked aluminum frame corners and profiles 

Interior Renovations & Finishing:

• Kitchen installation and remodeling
• Bathroom modifications and upgrades
• Tile installation on floors, showers, and backsplashes
• Cabinet and countertop installation
• Interior painting
• Plaster repair on ceilings and walls
• Drywall installation, patching, and finishing
• Flooring installation (tile, laminate, vinyl, hardwood, etc.)
• Interior finishing of the highest quality for trim, casings, and details

Structural & Leveling Work:

• House leveling where applicable
• Subfloor leveling to correct uneven floors and prepare for new finishes
• New openings for doors or windows
• Enlarged openings to improve layout, access, or natural light
• Structural reinforcement and framing corrections where required

Basement Projects:

• Basement water ingress repair and mitigation
• Basement finishing, including framing, insulation, drywall, flooring, and basic layouts 

General Home Repairs:

• Plumbing repairs
• Ectrical troubleshooting (switches, fixtures, small corrections)
• General handyman work inside and outside the home
• Deck repairs and exterior woodwork
• Rental turnover repairs for landlords
